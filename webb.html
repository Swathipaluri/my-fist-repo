Aditya Degree College is a private educational institution in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, established in 1998. It is affiliated with Andhra University and accredited by NAAC with a B++ grade. The college offers undergraduate programs in Management, Science, and Computer Applications. Key Features: Established: 1998. Affiliation: Andhra University. Accreditation: NAAC-B++. Location: Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Programs: Offers undergraduate degrees in Management, Science, and Computer Applications. Focus: Aims to develop professional, ethical, and innovative minds and focuses on the overall development of students. Infrastructure: Well-equipped with facilities like a gym, medical facilities, and partnerships with international universities. Industry Connections: Collaborations with Red Hat Academy, VMware IT Academy, and SAP Learning Hub. Placements: Aditya Degree College provides placement support through its Training & Placement Division. Campus Recruitment Training: Offers Aptitude and Communicative English training from the first day.
